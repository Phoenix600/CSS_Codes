CSS Background Size Property

background-repeat : repeat; (default)

background-size : auto; (default)

Displaying the image in its orignal dimensions and in remaing portion of the frame or body image is getting repeated

background-repeat : no-repeat;

background-size : auto; (default)

Displays the image in its orignal dimension wihtout any repeatition in remaing area of the frame

background-repeat : no-repeat;

background-size : contain;

Display the image by scaling up its ratio without cropping edges of the images

background-repeat : no-repeat;

background-size : cover;

Displays the full-size image by taking full width and height of frame and edges may get cropped.
